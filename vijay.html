vijay

Thalapathy Vijay, whose full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, is one of the most popular and influential actors in Tamil cinema. Born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, he began his career as a child artist in films directed by his father, S.A. Chandrasekhar, and made his debut as a lead actor with Naalaiya Theerpu in 1992. Over the years, he transformed from a romantic hero in movies like Poove Unakkaga and Kadhalukku Mariyadhai into a mass action star through blockbusters such as Ghilli, Thuppakki, Mersal, Master, and Leo. Known for his exceptional dance skills, charismatic screen presence, and strong fan base, Vijay has become one of the highest-paid actors in India with a global following. Apart from acting, he is also a playback singer and a philanthropist, contributing to causes like education and disaster relief. In 2024, Vijay entered politics by founding his own party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam, marking a new chapter in his career and aiming to serve people beyond cinema.
